The R-value of a missed call

You don't lose the call. You lose the callout.

For a trade, a missed call isn't a missed message — it's a customer with an urgent problem who books whoever answers first. That's a real emergency callout gone, every time.

Evenings & weekends

The calls come when you can't answer

Burst geysers, tripped mains, lockouts — the urgent, high-value jobs land after hours, exactly when you're on a tool or asleep.

No second chance

Most callers never leave a message

They don't wait or try again — they scroll to the next name and dial. If you're not answering, your competitor is.

On the job = off the phone

You can't quote and work at once

While your hands are busy, enquiries pile up unanswered — and the quotes you did send go cold without a follow-up.
