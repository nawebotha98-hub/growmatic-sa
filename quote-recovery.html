How Quote Recovery works

Log the quote once. We do the chasing.

No new software to learn and nothing to install. It works with the way you already quote — by WhatsApp, email or on paper.

01

You log the quote

Send us the customer, amount and number — a quick WhatsApp, email or one line in a shared sheet. That's your whole job done.

02

We follow up for you

A friendly, professional reminder goes out in your voice after a few days — and again if needed — so no quote is ever forgotten.

03

You see who's interested

Every reply is tracked. You get told exactly which customers are keen, so you can close them while they're warm.

04

We total the rands recovered

At month-end you get one clear number: the value of the jobs Quote Recovery won back for you. Proof, in rands.
