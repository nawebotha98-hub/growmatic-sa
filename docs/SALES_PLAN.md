# GrowMatic SA — Sales Plan

A practical 30/60/90-day plan for getting the first paying clients for GrowMatic SA (AI Automation Platform for South African Businesses).

---

## 1. What we're selling (the offer)

Don't sell "AI automation" — sell the outcome. Lead with one of these packaged offers:

| Package | What the client gets | Price (ZAR) — must match the website |
|---|---|---|
| **Lead Response Bot** (flagship) | AI agent answering WhatsApp, email & website chat 24/7, lead capture & follow-up, booking automation, Missed Call Text-Back, monthly report | R3,500/month + R5,000 once-off setup |
| **Admin Autopilot** | Everything in Lead Response Bot + automated quoting & invoice follow-ups, customer reminders, Client Dashboard, priority support | R4,500/month + R7,500 once-off setup |
| **Custom Automation Build** | Bespoke workflow automation (CRM, email, WhatsApp, sheets), business audit, staff training | From R15,000 once-off + custom retainer |

Anchor everything to one number: **"How many enquiries do you miss after hours, and what is one lost job worth?"** If a missed lead is worth R2,000+, a R3,500/month bot pays for itself with two saved leads.

## 2. Who we're selling to (target market)

Start narrow. Pick ONE vertical for the first 90 days so your message, case study, and referrals compound. Best-fit SA verticals for AI automation:

1. **Real estate agencies** — high lead volume, slow response times, every missed lead is a lost commission.
2. **Law firms & accountants** — expensive staff doing repetitive intake/admin.
3. **Medical/dental/aesthetic practices** — bookings, reminders, no-show reduction.
4. **Home services** (solar installers, security, plumbing) — after-hours enquiries, quote follow-up.
5. **E-commerce & retail** — order queries, WhatsApp support.

**Ideal client profile:** 5–50 staff, already getting leads (Google, Facebook, referrals), owner-managed, based in Gauteng / Western Cape / KZN metros.

## 3. How we reach them (channels, in order of ROI)

1. **Warm network first** — WhatsApp/call everyone you know who owns or manages a business. Ask for 15 minutes or an intro. This is where clients 1–3 come from.
2. **Cold email** — 20–30 personalised emails/day to owners in your chosen vertical (scripts in `EMAIL_SCRIPTS.md`). Source contacts from Google Maps, company websites, LinkedIn.
3. **LinkedIn** — connect with 10–15 owners/day in the vertical, short non-pitchy note, then move to email/call.
4. **WhatsApp follow-up** — after any reply or meeting, move the conversation to WhatsApp; in SA this closes deals faster than email.
5. **Proof content** — one short screen-recording demo (60–90s) of the bot answering a real enquiry. Attach/link it in every email.

> **POPIA note:** For B2B outreach, email business addresses obtained from public sources, identify yourself honestly, and include a clear opt-out line in every email. Stop contacting anyone who opts out. Never buy scraped consumer lists.

## 4. The sales process (enquiry → paid)

1. **Outreach** → goal is a 15-minute call, nothing more.
2. **Discovery call (15 min):** Ask — How do leads come in? Who answers them? What happens after hours? What's a customer worth? Don't demo yet.
3. **Live demo (20 min, within 48h):** Show the bot answering *their* type of enquiry — pre-build a demo using their website's info. This is the wow moment.
4. **Proposal (1 page, same day):** Problem → solution → price → start date. One option, one price, valid 7 days.
5. **Pilot close:** Offer a **30-day paid pilot** (e.g. setup fee waived, month-to-month, cancel anytime). Removes risk, gets a yes.
6. **Onboard in under a week**, then at day 25 review results and convert to a 6/12-month agreement.

## 5. 30 / 60 / 90-day plan

**Days 1–30 — Foundation & first clients**
- Pick one vertical and one flagship package.
- Build the 90-second demo video + one-page proposal template.
- Contact entire warm network (target: 50 conversations).
- Start cold email: 20/day, 4 days/week (~320 sent).
- **Goal: 10 discovery calls, 2–3 pilot clients (even discounted — you need proof).**

**Days 31–60 — Proof & pipeline**
- Turn pilot results into a one-paragraph case study with real numbers ("Agency X responded to 214 after-hours leads and booked 31 viewings").
- Add the case study to every email — reply rates roughly double with proof.
- Keep cold email at 20–30/day; add LinkedIn touches.
- Ask every happy client for 2 referrals (offer a referral reward, e.g. one free month).
- **Goal: 5–6 paying clients total.**

**Days 61–90 — Systemise & scale**
- Convert pilots to annual agreements.
- Automate your own outreach (you're an automation company — dogfood it).
- Raise prices for new clients once you have 3+ case studies.
- **Goal: 8–10 clients, ±R30k–R50k MRR, referral engine running.**

## 6. Weekly operating rhythm

| Day | Activity |
|---|---|
| Mon | Prospect list building (find 100 new contacts), send batch 1 |
| Tue–Thu | 20–30 cold emails/day, follow-ups, discovery calls |
| Fri | Demos, proposals, pipeline review, WhatsApp follow-ups |

**Track only 4 numbers:** emails sent, replies, calls booked, deals closed. If replies < 3% after 200 sends, change the subject line and first sentence — not the whole email.

## 7. Objection handling cheat-sheet

- **"AI will annoy my customers"** → "It hands over to a human the moment it's unsure — and right now, after-hours enquiries get no reply at all. Which annoys them more?"
- **"Too expensive"** → "What's one lost job worth? The bot pays for itself with X saved leads a month. And the pilot is month-to-month — cancel anytime."
- **"We already have a receptionist"** → "Perfect — this handles the 60% of repetitive queries so she can focus on the customers who need a human."
- **"Send me more info"** → Send the 90-second demo video + case study, then book the call before ending the conversation: "Great — I'll send it now. Does Thursday 10:00 work to walk you through it in 10 minutes?"
