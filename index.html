What We Build

Automation systems that move the needle.

Chat Agent

Automated customer service.

One system, three channels — WhatsApp, email, and a live chat widget embedded on your own website. Answers FAQs, qualifies leads, drafts on-brand email replies and follow-ups, and only escalates to you when a human actually needs to step in.

Lead Capture

Lead capture and follow-up.

Captures leads from any source and follows up automatically over WhatsApp and email until they book or buy.

Booking

Booking and appointments.

Customers self-book on WhatsApp in seconds. Automated reminders go out before every appointment so fewer people forget to show up.

Missed Call

Missed call text-back.

When you can't get to the phone, we auto-send the caller a WhatsApp message within seconds. A missed call turns into a live conversation instead of a lost customer — built for trades, salons, and anyone who can't stop what they're doing to answer.

Dashboard

Client dashboard.

A private, always-up-to-date link showing your upcoming bookings and leads — generated automatically from the same system that runs your WhatsApp. No extra app, no separate login, just one link that's always current.

Reputation

Review and reputation manager.

Automatically asks happy customers for a Google review at the right moment. Drafts on-brand replies to every review automatically, ready for you to approve with one tap.

Social

Social media automation.

Generates, schedules, and posts to Facebook and Instagram consistently — without you ever opening the app.

Custom

Custom automation workflows.

Any repetitive process mapped and automated: invoices, onboarding, reporting. If a human is repeating it, we replace it.
